TABLEAU DASHBOARD FOR COVID-19
PROJECT

In this project, we use Tableau to explore the trends in Global COVID Data. The visualizations not only facilitated a clearer understanding of the spread and impact of the virus but also provided stakeholders with an interactive and accessible platform for extracting actionable information. Through this Tableau-driven exploration, this project aimed to contribute to informed decision-making and public awareness regarding the evolving landscape of the global COVID-19 pandemic.
